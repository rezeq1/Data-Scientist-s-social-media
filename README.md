# Data Scientist's Social Media


A social media site for the data scientists . 
In the site you can :
1) Build your own model and use it .
2) Upload a datasets .
3) As a questions and get answered .
4) Share your models and datasets . 
5) Share posts about data science .
6) Search about datasets and models .
7) Follow the data scientist pages .

More and more actions you can do ... .
